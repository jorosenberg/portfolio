About Me

At heart I am a builder and creator. My passion peaks when I can create things that real people can use. Whether it's an app to find the right car, an automation pipeline to completely mask a database, or full-on architecture and design of a new product, trying to find the best way to make someone's life easier or more enjoyable is what drives me.

I have experience creating and maintaining architecture for the most part, but my software design and engineering skills are up to date and can support any project that requires them.

Currently getting myself up-to-date with the latest in AI and prompt engineering, including machine learning, and exploring how to use it to make better products and tools. I have a few AI powered projects that I am able to share currently.

I am always looking for new opportunities to learn and grow, so feel free to reach out about the latest in tech!
